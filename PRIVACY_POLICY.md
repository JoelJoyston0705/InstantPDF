# Privacy Policy for InstantPDF

**Last Updated:** December 10, 2024

## Introduction

InstantPDF ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** (for login and communication)
- **Name** (for personalization)
- **Password** (stored as encrypted hash, never in plain text)

### 2. Files You Upload
- **Temporary storage only** for conversion purposes
- **Automatically deleted** within 1 hour after conversion
- **Never stored permanently** on our servers
- **Not accessed** by our staff unless you report a technical issue

### 3. Automatically Collected Information
- **IP address** (for security and fraud prevention)
- **Browser type** (for compatibility)
- **Usage data** (for improving our services)
- **Cookies** (for session management)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
1. Provide and maintain our conversion services
2. Manage your account and authentication
3. Send important updates about the service
4. Improve our website and features
5. Prevent fraud and abuse
6. Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
- **User data:** Stored on secure servers (Railway/Vercel/Google Cloud)
- **Region:** Servers located in the United States (EU option available)
- **Protection:** Industry-standard encryption and security measures

### Security Measures
- Passwords hashed with bcrypt (one-way encryption)
- HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data transmission
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and authentication
- Secure database with regular backups

### File Handling
- Files processed in temporary memory
- Automatic deletion after conversion
- No permanent file storage
- No file content analysis or retention

## Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the right to:

### 1. Access Your Data
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

### 2. Rectification
Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.

### 3. Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
Request deletion of your account and all associated data.

### 4. Data Portability
Receive your data in a machine-readable format.

### 5. Object to Processing
Object to how we process your personal data.

### 6. Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

**To exercise these rights, email:** privacy@instantpdf.com

## Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained while your account is active
- **Uploaded files:** Deleted within 1 hour after conversion
- **Conversion history:** Deleted after 30 days
- **Deleted accounts:** All data permanently erased within 7 days

## International Data Transfers

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the EEA, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with GDPR.

## Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services:
- **Hosting:** Vercel, Railway, Google Cloud
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics (anonymized)
- **Payment:** Stripe (for premium subscriptions)

These services have their own privacy policies and are GDPR/CCPA compliant.

## Cookies

We use cookies for:
- **Essential:** Authentication and security
- **Functional:** Remember your preferences
- **Analytics:** Understand how you use our service

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 16 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
